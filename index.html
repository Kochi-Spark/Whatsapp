Spark Structure 
  1. Spark Regionals (Round 1): It is a quiz to test the student's aptitude and cognitive and mathematical reasoning skills, and it happens in over 20+ cities nationwide.
  2. Online Case Study (Round 2): The top participants from each city will qualify for Spark Round 2 and stand a chance to compete with students from different towns and diverse backgrounds. A problem statement will be provided to qualified participants for Round 2. Teams must come up with an innovative solution for the same.
  3. Spark Junior Conference (Round 3): Selected teams from Round 2 will be invited to IIT Madras on a two-day trip (accommodation will be provided). Finalists will participate in competitions and will get a chance to interact with IIT Madras Professors and students to ensure a holistic learning experience
